About MihaNion


My main activity is animation. Animation is the art of storytelling and one of the most accessible ways to share ideas with people in a simple and clear way. It’s something I truly value and respect. That’s why the main focus of my MihaNion project is promoting indie animation.

At the moment, the main MihaNion YouTube channel is not active, but that won’t last long—it will be active again soon.

However, the second channel, MihaNion Plus, is actively running. This channel is dedicated to Ukrainian entertainment content and cat memes. This format is very easy to understand and suitable for all ages. So if you like cats or are interested in Ukrainian content, the second channel might also interest you.


Another area of my work is comics. At the moment, the comics are still in development, and their release is planned on Instagram.

Right now, my Instagram only features my personal blog, but this is temporary—more content will be coming soon.

As for the comics themselves, they can be seen as the older brother of animation, since many popular cartoons were originally based on comics—and they remain interesting and relevant even today. That’s why this direction is also an important part of the MihaNion project.


Another area of my work, closely related to comics, is artwork. Recently, I started creating and sharing my own art on Threads and X (Twitter).

Original pieces, including works featuring my own characters or standalone illustrations, are published on Threads. Meanwhile, fan art is posted on X (Twitter).

However, it’s worth noting that special occasion artworks—such as holiday pieces or art marking important moments in my life or the channel’s journey—are shared on both platforms.


Do I only focus on art online? No.
I’m also a university student, and that’s where I got the idea to offer tutoring services. Since I’m currently in my first year, I provide tutoring at that level.

If you’d like to learn more about my tutoring, you can find additional details on my Instagram. This is also one of the activities of the MihaNion project.

For now, I work on it independently, but in the future I plan to expand and build a whole network of tutors to help young minds understand their studies and overcome learning difficulties.
