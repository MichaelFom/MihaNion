Story of MihaNion

Welcome to the official MihaNion website. My name is Michael Fomichov, and I am the creator of MihaNion. This is not just a cool nickname for social media, but a whole project I’m working on. For now, I’m developing it on my own, but in the future I plan to find people who, like me, love independent animation and see it not just as a way to make money, but as an art form with real value.

I joined YouTube in 2018, started working on animation in 2019, MihaNion was created in 2020, and in 2023 I turned it into a full project. You can learn more about it by navigating through the tabs located above..
